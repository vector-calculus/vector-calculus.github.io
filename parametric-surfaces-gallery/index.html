Basic Geometric Shapes

Plane

\begin{array}{l} x=1.5 u - 1 v + 1\\ y= u + v + 0.5\\ z= u +v + 1\\ \end{array}
-1.5\leq u \leq 1.5
-1.5\leq v \leq 1.5

Cylinder

\begin{array}{l} x=r\cos u\\ y=r\sin u\\ z=h\\ \end{array}
0\leq u \leq 2\pi
0\leq h \leq a,\,a\in \mathbb R

Sphere

\begin{array}{l} x = r\cos u \sin v\\ y = r\sin u \sin v\\ z = r\cos v\\ \end{array}
0\leq u \leq 2\pi
0\leq v \leq \pi

Pinecone

s = \dfrac{\pi}{2} \exp\left(-\dfrac{v}{8\pi}\right)
a = 1 - \dfrac{1}{2}\left[\frac{5}{4}\left( 1-\dfrac{\text{Mod}\left(3.6 v, 2\pi \right) }{2\pi}\right)^2 - \dfrac{1}{4}\right]^2
h = 1.95653 u^2 \left(1.27689 u -1\right)^2 \sin s
r = a \left(u \sin s + h \cos s\right)
x = r \sin v
y = r \cos v
z = a \left(u \cos s - h \sin s\right)
0\leq u \leq \pi
0\leq v \leq 17 \pi
